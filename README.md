# ft-atl-ai-tracing
Practical considerations for "embedded" AI traceability in the Atlassian eco-system of products.

## Atlassian & AI Traceability

We've all seen the productivity promise, but how best do you keep "tabs" on your agent's work.

What is available to you, as a product / project manager, or as an individual contributor to know if your AI teammates' contributions are effective?

What is available, when that isn't enough?

We dive into the practical detail of working alongside AI teammates and what you can do to keep them on the "straight and narrow" path.

This repo supports the [AI Traceability](https://ace.atlassian.com/events/details/atlassian-london-presents-from-data-center-to-ai-the-lloyds-cloud-migration-amp-ai-traceability/) talk given at Atlassian Commmunity Events, London on 22nd April 2026.
